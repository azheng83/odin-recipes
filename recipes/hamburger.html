Hamburger Recipe

Hamburgers are a common American dish that take on the appearance of a classical sandwich. While many are inspired by SpongeBob's krabby patty, many glutinous, obese, hot dog consuming rednecks still fail to create this televised delicacy.


Ingredients

  • Big, juicy buns
  • Warm, juicy meat
  • Lettuce (not the E. Coli kind)

  1. Warm up your pan and throw some beef patties on there
  2. Toast your buns
  3. Layer your meat with vegetables and sandwich it between the bread
