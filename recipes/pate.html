Pate a Choux

Description

Pate a Choux is a traditional French dish made of minced liver of some sort. It is commonly served with a sliced baguette, or crackers. It is considered a delicacy, and it has a pungent, yet savory flavour.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb chicken liver
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Put chicken in food processor and make sure it's almost liquid
  2. Incorporate ingredients such as onion and spices
  3. Serve finished product on a sliced baguette
